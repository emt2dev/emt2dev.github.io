Back in 2023, I joined a healthcare company as the sole developer on a project that had been running since 2021. After integrations and UI were working, I tackled database performance. At that point, fetching just ten patient records took over 35 seconds. By introducing database-side pagination with stored procedures and switching to a more efficient ORM, I reduced that to under one second.
